My Personal Interests

I really love to snowboard in the Winter. I learned how to snowboard when I was 10 years old and I've loved it ever since. I have three older brothers who also snowboard so they were able to teach me and help me get better. I really enjoy going fast down the mountain and manoeuvring through the trees when there is a lot of powder.

Video Games are one of my other favorite interests. I like to play video games with friends because it's a nice way to socialize and build relationships. The best part about it is that we can play together whenever we want. I have friends who live outside of Utah, but I'm still able to stay connected with them by playing video games online.

As you can tell from my Haiku above, I also like to read. My favorite genres are sci-fi and fantasy. It's always nice to settle down with a good book for a couple hours that will take you away to another world.
